Scenario:

The SOC team detected unusual activity on an employee’s Windows 10 workstation. A non-standard process was observed listening on a high TCP port late at night. The user account belongs to an employee who was recently placed under disciplinary review.

The workstation has been isolated, and a memory dump was captured before shutdown.

Your task: analyze the memory image and reconstruct what the user was doing at the time of acquisition.
